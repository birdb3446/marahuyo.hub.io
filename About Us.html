Company


Marahuyo came from the ancient Filipino word means to be enchanted


Marahuyo.hub is a clothing company established December 30, 2022 by the first year students of University of Makati. This is a static website for a clothing store and it based in the Philippines and are focusing more on selling local products of the country. Some of the products are inspired by other countries but all of the garments, clothes and even threads are proudly made here in the Philippines. Supporting our local products is the reason why this shop is made.


Marahuyo.hub is just a facebook page before but the programer and also the owner of the said facebook page, Jestin Clark V. Sacayle decided to use this as their basis in making the Final Project in their subject Intcom. It takes him almost 3 weeks to finish this static website. Together with his team, it is now a clothing website that will be used soon as their business.


Thank you for supporting our simple website and I hope you will wait for more updates and continue to support our website until it became fully functional.
